GLOBAL URBAN AIR MOBILITY

"Empowering cities' future with our solution for
implementing Urban Air Mobility (UAM) strategies"

GUAMOBILITY is a consulting ﬁrm positioned as a key player in supporting countries, territories, and cities in the development of Advanced Air Mobility (AAM) and Urban Air Mobility (UAM) strategies. Our focus is on sustainability, decarbonization, accessibility, and public acceptance, contributing to the reduction of GHG emissions and expanding accessibility to new urban mobility options. We ensure seamless intermodal integration with conventional transport systems.

By offering innovative, environmentally friendly, and acceptable solutions, GUAMOBILITY helps governments and cities optimally integrate advanced air mobility infrastructure into urban spaces, promoting cleaner, safer, and more sustainable mobility

To achieve these objectives, GUAMOBILITY has brought together a network of experts, consultants, and companies with complementary skills, ranging from urban planners and architects to aeronautical engineers and legal experts. Together, we deliver integrated AAM/UAM services, driving new socio-economic opportunities in what we call the "Low-Altitude Urban Economy."

SUPPORT THE DEVELOPMENT AND COMPLIANCE OF THE LOCAL AVIATION FRAMEWORK FOR UAM:

Developing UAM regulatory and operational frameworks, in harmony with the specific needs of each region or locality, including obtaining necessary authorizations in line with safety, security, privacy and sustainability requirements, as well as identification of suitable UAM operators and interface with them.

CONDUCTING PRE-FEASIBILITY STUDIES FOR URBAN AIR MOBILITY SYSTEMS:

Conducting in-depth analyses, based on traffic studies, socio-economic studies, and recommendations on economic models, to assess the viability and impact of urban air mobility projects.

MASTER PLANNING FOR DRONE SYSTEMS AND ACTION PLAN:

Designing detailed strategic plans for the integration of drones into urban infrastructure, identifying priority objectives, necessary resources, and key steps for implementation.

IMPLEMENTATION OF POCS AND DEMONSTRATORS ACCORDING TO THE MOST RELEVANT USE CASES FOR EACH TERRITORY:

Developing and testing prototypes and demonstrators of urban air mobility solutions, highlighting their utility and feasibility in specific urban contexts.

CONDUCTING DETAILED DEPLOYMENT STUDIES AND PROJECT EXECUTION MANAGEMENT:

Developing detailed operational plans and supervising the implementation of urban air mobility projects, ensuring compliance with quality and safety standards.

FEDERATION OF NATIONAL, REGIONAL, AND LOCAL ECOSYSTEMS AROUND URBAN AIR MOBILITY:

Establishing partnerships and synergies among governmental, industrial, and community stakeholders to develop urban air mobility solutions tailored to each territory.

USER INTERACTION POINT:

Establishing a strategy for transparent communication and close collaboration with end-users, local authorities, and relevant stakeholders, to proactively address their needs and concerns, and optimize the acceptance and adoption of urban air mobility solutions.
